Translation

Real Time Translation

Real-time translation with voice recognition is the technology that can help you translate one language to another on the fly on either male or female voice. With the latest neural machine translation (NMT) platforms, two people can have a conversation in different languages with minimal delays and near perfect accuracy.

Transcription

Real Time transcript

Real Time Transcript is a highly intuitive feature, which records all the conversation between Doctors and Patients as they speak. This enables a ready to use document for referring to for any recommendation now or in future.

EMR

EMR Dictation

Electronic medical records or EMRs can be generated by dictation. There is no need to key-in. The record can be sent to its required locations and is preserved for any future consultation.
